Exports, Imports, and the Cost of Clothing

a visualization of imports and exports in the apparel industry

Every other bundle of goods in the consumer price index has increased in price over time -- except for clothing and communication. While there are some obvious hypothetical explanations for why the cost of communication has decreased (the internet, namely), it is harder to explain the decrease in the cost of clothing. This project uses data from data source and data source to look at the change in apparel imports from and exports to China, as a beginning point to suggest that globalization has been key in keeping the cost of clothing low.
